  • About Wohin? Records

    Founded with a simple question — “Wohin?” (Where to?) — the label champions virtual artists - the result of emerging artificial intelligence technologies. Artificial intelligence is not just a new way to create music, it is a new way to experience music. Our approach is to create music with the same care and attention to detail as we would with a real artist. It is about quality, not quantity.
